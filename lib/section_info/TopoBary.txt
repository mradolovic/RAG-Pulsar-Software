Computes the topocentric pulse period of B0329+54 for a specific observation. It uses the Astropy library to calculate the Earth's radial velocity toward the pulsar at the time and location of the observation, converts this to a Doppler frequency shift in ppm, then applies it to the current barycentric period (extrapolated from the ATNF catalogue P0, P1, and epoch values) to give the period that will actually be observed at the telescope. The resulting topocentric period is fed directly into pulsar_det_an as the centre of the period search.
